Company Profile

Founded in April, 2016, with passion and vision, RichHealth integrates biomedicine, high-end electronic component design, global marketing experts and dedicates in developing high-end medical device products.

RichHealth merged Micro Nipple Technology in the 3rd quarter of 2016. Integrating its micro-needle technology, RichHealth keeps developing the method of mass production and disposable minimally invasive continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) products and specializes in a total solution for disposable medical electronic system.

The first generation of wireless disposable temperature patch has been launched into the market. With interactive mobile APP, RichHealth has built an all-round uninterrupted proactive medical warning networking system.

The company is currently focusing on the development of high-tech, high value-added and high-market growth of continuous glucose monitoring system medical equipment product development. This product allows users to easily monitor physiological metabolic changes and feedback messages. Meanwhile, the results of the measurement will be analyzed by big data. The goal is to establish a health care platform for digital medical and cloud services to help users manage their blood sugar more easily.
