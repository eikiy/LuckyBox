About our purpose of Wi-Fi service.

We are a team formed by foreigners in Japan.
After many times of traveling in Japan, we noticed that there is a significant need within a trip for unlimited, fast, simple, and stable portable internet via a Wi-Fi device.

During trips in Japan, we always encountered challenges such as: Difficulties navigating without internet.
Language barriers.
Limited network access.

After years of development with a strong group of engineers, not only does our product connect to the local network seamlessly, but also contains translate function inside and can be used as a power bank.

Finally, our solution provides an answer to all these problems!

We deeply want to provide a service for not only ourselves, but also to travelers from all over the world, with a high quality Wi-Fi experience.
