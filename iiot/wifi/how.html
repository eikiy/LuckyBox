HOW IT WORKS

EASY AND FAST!

  • 1.Book online

  • 2.Delivery / Pickup

  • 3.Enjoy Internet

  • 4.Return

  • 1.Book online

    1- Complete the booking form

    2- Online payment

    We accept credit card & Pay-pal.

    3-Confirmation

    Receive the confirmation email then you are ready to go.

  • 2.Delivery / Pickup

    1-Specific home address

    Please ensure that there is someone able to receive the Wi-Fi machine for you with the provided address.
    *The shipping fee is dependent upon where it is being delivered. Please check through application.

    2 - Convenience Store of Post Office (Around 50,000 locations)

    Collect your Wi-Fi package in post offices or convenience stores after you arrive in Japan, by simply showing your tracking number and your passport to the counter.
    *Tracking number will be sent by email after it has been shipped.

    Hotel

    Collect the package at the front desk during check in.
    Please ensure that the receiver’s name is matched.

    Airport

    Collect at the airport.
    * A collection at the airport may incur an additional charge of $5 in addition to the delivery fee.

  • 3.Enjoy Internet

    1 - Turn on the Wi-Fi device

    by pressing on the Power button for 5 seconds .

    2 - Connect Wi-Fi

    Select the Wi-Fi and enter the password.

    3 - Enjoy high speed internet!

  • 4.Return easily

    1 - Pack the following items into the pouch.

    □ Wi-Fi Router
    □ AC Adaptor
    □ USB Cable
    Please make sure the items are all in the pouch.

    2 - Place it in the letter-pack envelope

    and seal it up to make sure the pouch does not fall out.

    3 - Drop the envelope in post box

    Simply drop the envelope in any Japan Post box.
    *If returned at an airport There is no post box after security check and CANNOT return by other company’s return box. So please return it before you pass the security check.
