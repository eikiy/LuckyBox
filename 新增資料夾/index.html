About Us

We Are Cake prides itself on customer satisfaction. "Making People happy" isn't just our tag line it is how we want every one of our customer to feel when they choose us for the special events in their lives. Create a special memory with one of our custom cakes.

We Are Cake was founded as a way to combine creativity, hard work and a passion for baking quality cakes and cupcakes. In the beginning it started with a simple desire to make family and friends events special with hand made delicious cakes creatively decorated to match one-of-a-kind themes. After multiple requests from friends and relatives to do the same for their events lead to the realization that not only did people love the cakes but we loved to do it for them. This has been the perfect recipe for a successful business.
